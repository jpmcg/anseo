Tranquility in Dunfanaghy

We have four double en-suite bedrooms. Two of the bedrooms are on the ground floor, making access easy for the less mobile. Both of these rooms have double French doors opening out onto separate courtyards.

All the rooms are en-suite, feature a large king-size bed, with high quality Sealy mattresses, a flat-screen television, hair dryer, and tea and coffee making facilities. There is free wifi throughout the house. The Studio also has a small, fully equipped kitchen.

As well as everything you would expect to find in a bedroom, we have strived to provide a desk, or table, where those inclined to do so, might take up their quill, or cursor, and write. Perhaps their forthcoming novel, or poem. Or maybe simply a postcard home.

We have also provided a place in each room, where those inclined, may take up their corkscrew, and enjoy a quiet glass of Beaujolais. And forget about the writing. And everything else.

The Studio

The Studio is on the ground floor and incorporates a dining table, and a small, fully equipped kitchen. It is available for booking all year round, even when the B&B is closed.
