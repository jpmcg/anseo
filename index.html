Luxury Bed & Breakfast Dunfanaghy

We are very proud of Anseo, our Bed & Breakfast on the outskirts of the picturesque village of Dunfanaghy in north-west Donegal, in one of the most rugged, scenic and fascinating parts of Ireland, and ideally situated to explore the many beaches, mountains, forests and local culture for which the area is famous.
