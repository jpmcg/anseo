Breakfast Menu

Set yourself up for the day, with a delicious Anseo Breakfast - the perfect start to any day.

As well as offering a traditional Donegal breakfast, using local dry-cured bacon, handmade sausages and free-range eggs, we would like to tempt you with several interesting alternatives:

  • Freshly baked, homemade bread and scones
  • Pancakes served with blueberries, lemon and maple syrup
  • French toast with local, Donegal, dry-cured, bacon on the side
  • Scrambled free-range eggs with cream, chives and smoked salmon, incorporating the most incredible, organic, turf-smoked salmon - smoked locally, using local turf, by young, knowledgeable and enthusiastic artisans. Absolutely superb!

We also offer vegetarian variations of any of the above, and lots more besides. However, if there is something in particular you would like for breakfast, then, with a modicum of advance notice, we will be delighted to do our best to accommodate

Breakfast Times

Basically, we serve breakfast between 8.30 am and 10.00 am, but, if for some reason that does not suit, then please let us know.
